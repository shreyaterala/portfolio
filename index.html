Haptic Museum Display

Fusion 360 | Arduino | Fabrication


Problem: Museums have limited accessibility for those with visual impairments.

Solution: A tactile display device consisting of a 7x5 pin array. The display conveys shape through the pattern of actuated pins and color through the height of corresponding pins.

Key Contributions:

  • Custom CAM design and tight tolerances to ensure stability.
  • Designed for full-hand exploration referencing a height-color key.
